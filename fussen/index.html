About us

This house is located in the city centre of Füssen, approximately 5 minutes walk from the train station and 3 min from the tourist information centre. You'll enjoy beautiful views from the house to Neuschwanstein castle and to the Allgäuer alpine mountains. It is ideal for families, small groups, couples and individual travelers who are exploring Füssen, the Allgäu region and the castles. It is our pleasure to welcome you and to have you as our guests. We provide free parking space, free Wi-fi and laundry service! What are you waiting for?
